About Tilottama Campus

Tilottama Campus was established in 1996 as the first private college in Rupandehi by a team of dedicated, experienced, and enterprising academicians to meet the growing demands of parents and students in higher education.

Since its establishment, it runs 10+2 programmes in Science and Management affiliated with NEB and BBA programmes affiliated with Pokhara University.

The college is located in Butwal, Yogikuti, offering peaceful surroundings, modern labs, library, sports facilities, and classrooms under CCTV surveillance.

Vision

To be an admired center of educational excellence nurturing globally-centric, socially sensitive professionals with ethical and moral attributes.

Objectives

Provide practical, value-based quality education, generate ethical behavior, offer disciplined & friendly atmosphere.
